## May 2023 release

In this release, new application URLs are available for Merative Integrated Care, Connect Individuals, Connect Providers, and IBM Cognos Analytics, as applicable. An email including the new URLs and instructions on how to bookmark them has been sent to you.

A new URL is also available for the reporting data catalog: https://merative.github.io/integratedcare-reporting-datacatalog. Bookmark this URL for use going forward.

For more information, see the [May 2023 release notes](/pdf/wcm/MerativeIntegratedCare_ReleaseNotes_May2023.pdf) on the release notes page.

We are pleased to announce our new product name, Merative Integrated Care.

* Connect Individuals
* Connect Providers

## September 2022 release

In this release, we added further views to the bring-your-own-tool reporting solution, and we introduced a new security role resource: Delete Notes.

We also clarified the documentation that relates to client deletion notifications. Under data privacy regulations, you are automatically subscribed to client status data notifications, which notify you about client deletions in Watson Care Manager.
